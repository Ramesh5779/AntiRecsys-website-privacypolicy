Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2025

1. Data Collection

We operate on a "Local First" principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, search history, or usage habits to any external servers.

AntiRecsys is designed to protect your privacy. We have no servers, no databases, and no analytics that track your behavior.

2. Local Storage

Any preferences you save (such as your daily goal or notification settings) and your anonymous usage statistics are stored strictly locally on your device using Android's secure internal storage.

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Third-Party Privacy

When you use our App to open a third-party platform (like Instagram, YouTube, or Reddit), their privacy policies apply to your activity on their app.

We do not have access to your data once you leave our App. We simply provide a deep link to open your chosen search in the target application.

4. Permissions

Notifications: We request notification permissions solely to provide you with session reminders and quick-action controls. You can disable notifications at any time in Settings.

We do not request access to your contacts, camera, microphone, location, or any other sensitive permissions.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone, including children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
